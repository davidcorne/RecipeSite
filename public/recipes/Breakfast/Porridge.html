# Porridge # This is my simple microwave porridge recipe from reading online recipes and tweaking them. ## Ingredients ## - ⅜ cup oats - ⅜ cup milk - ⅜ cup water - 1 tsp brown sugar - Toppings ## Method ## 1. Mix the non-topping ingredients in a microwave safe bowl. 2. Microwave for 1.30, then stir. 3. Heat again for 0.30, then stir. 4. Heat again for 0.30. 5. Add toppings.
