# Chinese Curry Sauce # This was made with ingredients inspired by [this](http://allrecipes.co.uk/recipe/28375/chinese-curry-sauce.aspx) but method and amounts modified. ## Ingredients ## - 1½ pints chicken stock - 3 tablespoons cornflour (mixed with sufficient cold water to make a paste) - 2 tablespoon soy sauce - 1 teaspoon Chinese five spice powder - 1 teaspoon ground ginger - ½ teaspoon mild chilli powder - 4 teaspoons mild curry powder ## Method ## 1. Heat the stock until simmering. 2. Add the cornflour paste. 3. Add the rest of the spices.
