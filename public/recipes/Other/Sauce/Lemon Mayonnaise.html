# Lemon Mayonnaise # This is originally from [here](http://www.geniuskitchen.com/recipe/lime-or-lemon-mayonnaise-256771). ## Ingredients ## - 2 egg yolks - 1 teaspoon dijon mustard - 230 ml olive oil - 1 tablespoon lemon juice - 1 teaspoon lemon zest ## Method ## 1. Combine egg yolks, mustard and half the lemon juice in a blender or food processor until light and creamy. 2. With the blender still running, add the oil in a thin stream until the mixture thickens. 3. Stir in remaining lemon juice, and the zest. 4. Season to taste.
