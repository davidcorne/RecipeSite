# Curry Powder # Originally from [here](http://allrecipes.co.uk/recipe/1876/homemade-curry-powder.aspx). ## Ingredients ## - 2 tablespoons ground cumin - 2 tablespoons ground coriander - 1 tablespoon ground turmeric - ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper - ½ teaspoon mustard seed - ½ teaspoon ground ginger ## Method ## 1. Crush mustard seed. 2. Combine all the ingredients with the crushed mustard seeds. 3. Store in an airtight container.
