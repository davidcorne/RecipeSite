# Sweet Mustard Gammon # ## Ingredients ## ### Joint ### - unsmoked bacon joint - pepper - 1 tablespoon demerara sugar ### Glaze ### - 12 oz demerara sugar - juice of 1 lemon - 6 tablespoons water - 4 tablespoons mustard (mum uses slightly less) ## Method ## 1. Soak gammon in cold water overnight then rinse. 2. Calculate cooking time (20 to the pound then 20 minutes more). 3. Bring to the boil for half the time with pepper and sugar in the water. 4. Take out of water cut off rind and score the fat underneath. 5. Make glaze put all ingredients in microwave for around a minute. 6. Place in roasting tin (small). 7. Glaze and cook for the rest of the time at 180.
