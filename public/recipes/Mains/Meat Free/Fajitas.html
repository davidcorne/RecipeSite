# Vegetarian Fajitas # ## Ingredients ## - Fajitas kit - Courgette - Onion - Garlic - Peppers - Lettuce - Cucumber - Sour Cream - Grated Cheese ## Method ## 1. Fry the courgette. 2. Add the onion and garlic. 3. Add the peppers. 4. Add the spice mix. 5. Add the salsa. 6. Microwave 2 fajitas for 40 seconds. 7. Serve with sour cream, grated cheese and salad.
