# Dumplings # These are from [this recipe](http://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/brazilian-pork-stew-corn-dumplings) for brazilian pork stew. ## Ingredients ## - 100g cold butter, diced - 200g self-raising flour - 140g cornmeal or finely ground polenta, plus extra for dusting - Â½ tsp bicarbonate of soda - 140g sweetcorn, from a can, drained, or freshly cut from a cob (just boil for 3 mins first) - 75ml buttermilk (75ml milk + 1 tsp lemon juice, let sit for 5-10 minutes) - 1 medium egg, beaten ## Method ## 1. Rub the butter into the flour until it resembles fine crumbs, then stir in the cornmeal, bicarb and sweetcorn. 2. Mix in the buttermilk and all but 1 tbsp of the egg to make a soft dough. 3. Season with some salt and roll the mixture into 12 soft dumplings, then roll in a little more cornmeal to coat the tops. 4. Brush the tops with the reserved beaten egg. 5. Sit 6 of the dumplings on top of a stew and the rest on a baking tray lined with baking parchment. 6. Put both in the oven and cook for 25 mins until the dumplings are golden and risen.
