# Apple Coleslaw # ## Ingredients ## - Thinly sliced white cabbage - Spiralised carrot - Thinly sliced red onion - Thick sliced pink apple - Full fat mayo (small amount) - Salt and pepper ## Method ## 1. Mix all fruit and vegetables together. 2. Add small amounts of mayo and increase as you don't want it drenched otherwise it goes soggy. 3. Season to taste.
