# Victoria Sponge # ## Ingredients ## - 170g soft marge - 170g caster suger - 170 self-raising four - 3 eggs ## Method ## 1. Preheat oven to 160 degrees. 2. Prepare 2x 20cm buttered lined tins. 3. Soften butter. 3. Cream butter and suger in a food processor. 4. Add the eggs and flour. 5. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
