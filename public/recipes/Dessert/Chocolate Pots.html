# Chocolate Pots # ## Ingredients - 100g dark chocolate - 250ml double cream - 40g muscovado sugar - vanilla - 1 egg ## Method 1. blend chocolate 2. cream, sugar in pan and bring to the boil 3. pour into blender leave for 1 minute 4. switch on and add egg for 20 seconds 5. pour into ramekins and chill (2hours or preferably overnight)
