# MVC Sticky Toffee Pudding # ## Ingredients ## - 2 oz margarine - 6 oz granulated sugar - 8 oz self-raising flour - 1 tsp baking powder - 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda - 1 tsp vanilla extract - 1 egg - 6 oz chopped dates - 1/2 pint of water ## Method ## 1. Boil water & dates together. 2. Mix other ingredients together. 3. Add date & water mix. 4. Put in greased tin & cook on 180 for about 40 mins. 5. Cut cake as soon as cooked and pour over sticky toffee sauce.
